01 / PHILOSOPHY

The Art of Reduction

We believe that architectural quality is not achieved by adding details, but by removing everything that is non-essential. Space is defined not by the walls that enclose it, but by the light, shadows, and materials that dwell within.

Atelier Monolith operates at the intersection of modern minimalism and raw brutalist materiality. Our designs respond to context, gravity, and the flow of time. We craft monolithic residences, cultural hubs, and spatial installations that evoke emotion through structural purity.

04 / THE STUDIO

Shaping the Form

Founded in 2012 by architects Hans Klemens and Yua Sato, Atelier Monolith has grown into a collaborative workspace of 18 designers, engineers, and researchers. With studios in Berlin and Tokyo, our works span Europe and Asia, sharing a common vision of architectural silence.
